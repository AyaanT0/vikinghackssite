What is VikingHacks?

We, at North Park Hack Club, are so excited for you to join us for VikingHacks 2023, a low-barrier, beginner-friendly hackathon to provide you with an opportunity to gain more hackathon experience in an inclusive and accessible environment!

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A technology-focused event where teams create new projects. You can build anything you want, whether that be a mobile app, a website, or anything else!

Do I need to know how to code?

Students of all skill levels are encouraged to attend, even if youâ€™ve never programmed before. VikingHacks is beginner friendly and we will host various workshops throughout the weekend that can help you get started.

Who can attend?

The first step to attend would be to register. Undergraduate students are encouraged to register but we would also love for high schoolers to join us! If you are under 18 and plan to attend, your parent or guardian must sign a waiver (which will be available soon).

Do I have to pay?

VikingHacks is completely free thanks to our sponsors.

What do I bring?

You need a valid ID card for admission. Bring a laptop, charger, and a positive attitude. Everything else will be provided!

Can I come without a team?

Of course! You will be paired with other participants who also don't have teams. Take this opportunity to meet new people and bond with them over your project!
